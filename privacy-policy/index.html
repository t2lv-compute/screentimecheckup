Privacy Policy

Screen Time Checkup — Last updated: February 3, 2026

TL;DR Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or sell any personal information.

Overview

Screen Time Checkup is a productivity app that helps you stay aware of how you spend your time. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. All information you enter into the app, including:

  • Activity tags (focus activities and distractions)
  • Check-in logs (what you're doing, what you should be doing, importance scores)
  • App settings and preferences

is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send periodic check-in reminders. You can disable these in your device settings at any time.
  • Exact Alarms: To schedule check-in reminders at precise intervals.

These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality and not for tracking or advertising purposes.

Data Storage

On Android, data is stored in the app's private storage directory. On the web, data is stored in your browser's local storage. You can export your data as a JSON backup file and import it on another device if desired.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no SDKs that collect or transmit your data.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time using the "Clear Logs" and "Reset Tags" options in the app's Data Management section. Uninstalling the app will also remove all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is a general productivity tool suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
